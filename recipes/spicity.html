Roast Beef Horseradish Roll-Ups

Description

They are a quick, savory appetizer made by spreading a creamy horseradish mixture onto slices of roast beef, then rolling them up into bite-sized pieces. They're flavorful, protein-rich, and perfect for parties, snacks, or holiday gatherings.

Ingredients

  • Thinly sliced roast beef
  • Cream cheese
  • Prepared horseradish
  • Green onions
  • Fresh parsley

Steps

  1. Prepare the filling: Mix softened cream cheese, prepared horseradish, chopped green onions, and parsley until smooth.
  2. Spread the mixture: Evenly spread a thin layer of the horseradish cream cheese over each slice of roast beef.
  3. Roll them up: Tightly roll each slice of roast beef into a log.
  4. Chill and slice: Refrigerate the roll-ups for 20–30 minutes, then slice into bite-sized pieces if desired.
  5. Serve: Arrange on a platter and garnish with fresh parsley or chives before serving.

Italian Subs-Restaurant Style

Description

They are hearty, deli-style sandwiches layered with a variety of Italian cured meats, provolone cheese, crisp lettuce, juicy tomatoes, onions, and a tangy Italian dressing, all served on a fresh, crusty sub roll. They're packed with bold flavors and are perfect for lunch or a satisfying dinner.

Ingredients

  • Italian sub rolls
  • Salami
  • Ham
  • Provolone Cheese
  • Lettuce

Steps

  1. Prepare the bread: Slice the sub rolls lengthwise and lightly toast them if desired.
  2. Layer the fillings: Add provolone cheese, then layer the salami, ham, and any other Italian meats.
  3. Add the vegetables: Top with shredded lettuce, tomato slices, red onion, and banana peppers.
  4. Season and dress: Drizzle with olive oil, red wine vinegar, or Italian dressing, then sprinkle with oregano, salt, and black pepper.
  5. Assemble and serve: Close the sandwich, press it gently, slice in half if desired, and serve immediately.

Italian Subs-Restaurant Style

Description

they are hearty deli sandwiches filled with Italian meats, provolone cheese, fresh vegetables, and zesty dressing on a crusty sub roll. Perfect for a quick, satisfying meal.

Ingredients

  • Italian sub rolls
  • Salami and sliced ham
  • Provolone cheese
  • Lettuce, tomatoes, and red onions
  • Italian dressing with oregano

Steps

  1. Slice the sub rolls and lightly toast them if desired.
  2. Layer the provolone cheese, salami, and ham inside the rolls.
  3. Add lettuce, tomato slices, and thinly sliced red onions.
  4. Drizzle with Italian dressing and sprinkle with oregano.
  5. Close the sandwich, cut in half, and serve immediately.
